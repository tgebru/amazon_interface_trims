You will be shown two different types of cars with three example images per car. The first car will be on the top and the second one at the bottom. You can hover on the images to make them bigger. The task is to specify whether the two cars are visually indistinguishible or not. Some cars look very similar but only differ in very subtle cases. If the two cars are visually indistinguishible press YES. If the two cars are visually different press NO and describe the differences. Please look at the examples below. Note that color differences do not count--if two cars look the same except for a difference in color they are considered to be visually the same. Other differences that don't count are sunroof, spoilers, tires and foglihgts. Below is a car image with labeled parts to aid you in identifying differences in parts
